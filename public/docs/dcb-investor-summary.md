# Discharge Bridge — Investor Summary

**Source:** `portmi3-ai/discharge-bridge-media/marketing/investor-summary.md`

## One-line description

Discharge Bridge is governed interoperability infrastructure for hospital-to-post-acute patient transitions. It coordinates clinical needs, payer constraints, and bed availability through deterministic orchestration, tenant-scoped infrastructure, and advisory AI.

## Opportunity

Hospital discharge to post-acute care is still run on fax, phone, and spreadsheets. Delays cost hospitals an estimated $2,500-$4,000 per patient per day in avoidable bed-days, strain ED capacity, and damage SNF relationships.

Discharge Bridge is designed to reduce placement coordination from days toward minutes through deterministic routing infrastructure, subject to validation during enterprise pilot deployments.

## What we built

- Multi-tenant healthcare orchestration platform
- HL7/FHIR ingestion and normalization
- Deterministic SNF matching and routing
- Real-time bed availability verification
- Prior authorization preparation workflow
- Hospital, SNF, and admin portals
- Operator/trust APIs for enterprise reviewers
- Vertex AI advisory intelligence with deterministic workflows as source of truth

## Market

- Current commercial scope: U.S. hospital-to-post-acute coordination
- Initial SAM target: $600M-$1.2B
- Global vision scope: healthcare logistics infrastructure network
- U.S. universe: 6,000+ hospitals and 15,000+ SNFs
- Priority sequence: Michigan-first, Midwest, national

## Business model

- Pilot: $30k-$50k per hospital over 60-120 days
- Production license: $75k-$175k+ annual
- Expansion: per-facility uplift, analytics, behavioral health, home health, payer modules
