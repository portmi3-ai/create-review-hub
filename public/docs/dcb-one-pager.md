# Discharge Bridge One-Pager

**Source:** `portmi3-ai/discharge-bridge-media/marketing/one-pager.md`

## AI-Assisted Discharge Orchestration for Hospitals

Discharge Bridge automates hospital-to-post-acute transitions by connecting hospital EMRs, payer workflows, and SNF networks in one secure platform.

## Problem

Discharge delays create cascading operational and financial damage:

- Emergency department congestion
- Inpatient bed shortages
- Payer authorization delays
- Care team burnout

Hospitals can lose $2,500-$4,000 per patient per day when placement is delayed.

## Solution

Discharge Bridge is a healthcare logistics orchestration platform built for discharge speed, compliance, and throughput.

## Core capabilities

- Clinical summarization and discharge readiness support using Vertex AI
- SNF capability matching and referral routing
- Real-time bed availability verification and inventory controls
- Prior authorization preparation workflows
- Multi-tenant portals for hospitals, SNFs, and admins
- Interoperability with Epic, MEDITECH, and Cerner / Oracle Health

## How it works

1. Hospital EMR sends referral through HL7 or FHIR
2. Interoperability layer normalizes clinical payloads
3. Vertex AI generates structured clinical insights
4. Routing engine identifies best-fit SNF options
5. Bed availability is confirmed in real time
6. Teams execute intake and placement through secure portals

## Commercial model

- Pilot: $30k-$50k per hospital over 60-120 days
- Production: $75k-$175k+ annual licensing
- Pricing scales by bed count, discharge volume, and activated modules

## Strategic positioning

Discharge Bridge sits at the intersection of healthcare interoperability, hospital logistics automation, and AI clinical workflow orchestration. It is positioned as healthcare infrastructure, not a point solution.
