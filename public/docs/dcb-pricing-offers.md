# DCB Pricing and Offer Stack

**Source:** `portmi3-ai/discharge-bridge-media/marketing/brand-os/Offers & Proof/pricing-and-offers.md`

## Entry offer: pilot program

- Price range: $30k-$50k per hospital
- Typical duration: 60-120 days
- Goal: prove throughput, placement speed, and operational fit

## Core offer: production platform license

- Annual license range: $75k-$175k+
- Pricing factors:
  - Hospital bed count
  - Annual discharge volume
  - Activated modules

## Expansion and upsell paths

- Multi-facility rollout across a health system
- Advanced analytics and benchmarking modules
- Additional transition pathways such as behavioral, home health, and payer integrations

## Pilot package should include

- Integration kickoff
- Initial workflow configuration
- Role-based portal onboarding
- Baseline KPI setup and pilot scorecard

## Production package should include

- Multi-site governance model
- SLA and support tiers
- Security and compliance packet
- Executive reporting cadence

## Pricing documentation rules

- Keep list pricing and negotiated pricing separate
- Use ranges publicly
- Maintain deal-specific terms in CRM/contracts
- Tie pricing narrative to measurable operational outcomes

## Proof requirements

For each customer stage, capture baseline metrics, midpoint pilot readout, final outcomes, and expansion recommendation.
