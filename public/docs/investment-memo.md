# Investment Memo — Discharge Bridge

## Thesis

Discharge Bridge should be evaluated as a healthcare infrastructure platform, not a point-solution dashboard.

## Customer

The initial customer profile is hospital operations, case management, care coordination, and discharge leadership.

## Market pain

Manual discharge workflows create avoidable inpatient days, case-management burden, SNF placement friction, payer readiness issues, and patient/family dissatisfaction.

## Differentiation

- Governance-aware workflow design
- Deterministic routing and auditability
- Advisory AI instead of uncontrolled automation
- SNF matching and readiness intelligence
- Patent-pending placement/resource matching method

## Risks

- Enterprise integration timelines
- Pilot-to-contract conversion
- Competitive response from incumbents
- Security and compliance maturity
