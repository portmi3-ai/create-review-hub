# Investor Pitch Deck — Discharge Bridge

## Positioning

Discharge Bridge is governance-aware discharge orchestration infrastructure for hospital-to-SNF transitions.

## Problem

Hospitals lose time and capacity when medically ready patients wait on post-acute placement, documentation completion, prior-auth readiness, and medication handoff coordination.

## Product

The platform coordinates discharge readiness, facility matching, documentation routing, SNF communication, medication coordination, and advisory AI while preserving deterministic workflow controls.

## Investor thesis

The initial wedge is hospital-to-SNF discharge orchestration. The broader platform opportunity is healthcare transition infrastructure across hospitals, post-acute providers, payers, and care coordination workflows.
